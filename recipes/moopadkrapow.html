Moo Pad Krapow!

Description:

Moo Pad Krapow is a Thai stir-fry dish made with ground pork, garlic, chilies, and Thai basil, seasoned with savory sauces and typically served over rice with a fried egg on top.

This dish is a staple of Thai street food, beloved for its bold, spicy flavor and quick preparation. It has roots in central Thailand and showcases the influence of Chinese stir-frying techniques blended with Thai herbs and spices.

Ingredients:

  • Ground Pork
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Fish Sauce
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Fresh Chilies
  • Thai Holy Basil

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and chilies; stir-fry until fragrant.
  3. Add ground pork and cook until it's no longer pink.
  4. Pour in sauces (soy sauce, fish sauce, oyster sauce, and a little sugar); stir well.
  5. Add Thai basil and cook until just wilted.
  6. Serve hot over rice, ideally with a crispy fried egg on top.
