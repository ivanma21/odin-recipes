Pad Thai!

Description:

Pad Thai is a flavorful Thai stir-fried noodle dish with rice noodles, eggs, tofu or shrimp, and peanuts, all coated in a sweet and tangy sauce.

Pad Thai was created after World War II to encourage the use of local ingredients like rice noodles and to promote national unity.

Ingredients:

  • Rice Noodles
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Green Onions
  • Crushed Peanuts
  • Lime
  • Your Favourite Protein Source
  • Fish Sauce
  • Tamarind sauce
  • Sugar
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Cooking Oil

Steps:

  1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions, then set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, then saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add eggs and scramble until cooked.
  4. Add protein (chicken, shrimp, or tofu) and cook until done.
  5. Mix in noodles, bean sprouts, and green onions.
  6. Pour sauce (fish sauce, tamarind paste, sugar, chili flakes) over everything and stir.
  7. Serve with lime wedges and crushed peanuts on top.
